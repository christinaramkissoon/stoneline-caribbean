THE STONELINE ADVANTAGE

Adding stucco to walls, especially in tropical climates, offers key benefits due to its unique properties.

Exceptional Durability

Rigid, impact-resistant material that averages a 50-100 year lifespan.

Improved Weather Resistance

Prevents moisture-related damage and extends paint lifespan in high humidity and frequent rain.

UV Resistance

Highly resistant to intense tropical UV rays for better colour retention and heat resistance.

Biological & Rugged Resistance

Reduced susceptibility to mold and algae. Ideal for harsh sea blast zones and dusty environments.
